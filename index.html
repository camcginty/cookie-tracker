The Cookie Brigade's Cookie Tracker

What is the Cookie Brigade?

We're a fundraising group for Child's Play Charity. We bake to distribute at gaming conventions while collecting donations to pass on to Child's Play. We've raised over $450k for them since our start in 2007.

  • Cookie Brigade main website
  • Child's Play website

Why a cookie tracker?

There are lots of gamers to feed at each convention and they are sad when we don't have cookies. If we know what we have coming, we know what and how much more we need.
